Recommendations for New Cat Owners

Category Recommendation
Food Choose a high-quality dry or wet cat food appropriate for your catâ€™s age.
Litter Box Scoop daily and clean the entire box at least once a week. One box per cat + one extra is ideal.
Vet Visits Schedule an initial check-up and keep up with annual vaccinations and exams.
Scratching Posts Provide scratching posts or pads to protect your furniture and keep claws healthy.
Toys Offer a variety of toys (balls, feathers, lasers) to keep your cat mentally and physically active.
ID & Microchip Make sure your cat wears a collar with an ID tag and consider microchipping.
Patience Give your cat time to adjust to their new home. Let them come to you on their terms.
